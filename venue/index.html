Venue

Braga

One of the oldest portuguese cities, established in Roman times as Bracara Avgvsta, Braga is nowadays a young city, full of traditions and places to visit.

Theatro Circo

Theatro Circo is a 20th-century, Portuguese revivalist theatre and one of the most emblematic theatre in Europe. It is located in one of the principal avenues in Braga, Avenida da Liberdade.
